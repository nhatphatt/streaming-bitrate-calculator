Your upload speed determines the maximum bitrate you can stream reliably. A good rule is to use only **70–75%** of your available upload bandwidth for live streaming.

## Quick answer table

| Streaming Setup | Minimum Upload Speed | Comfortable Upload Speed |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 720p 30fps | 5 Mbps | 6–8 Mbps |
| 720p 60fps | 7 Mbps | 8–10 Mbps |
| 1080p 30fps | 8 Mbps | 10–12 Mbps |
| 1080p 60fps | 10 Mbps | 12–20 Mbps |
| 1440p 60fps | 18 Mbps | 20–30 Mbps |
| 4K 30fps | 30 Mbps | 35–50 Mbps |
| 4K 60fps | 45 Mbps | 50–75 Mbps |

## Why upload speed matters

If your bitrate is too high for your connection, your stream can drop frames, buffer, and become unstable.
