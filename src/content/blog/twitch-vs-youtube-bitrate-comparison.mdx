Twitch and YouTube handle streaming bitrate very differently. Twitch is more restrictive, while YouTube allows higher bitrate and higher-resolution streams more easily.

## Twitch vs YouTube bitrate limits

For many streamers, Twitch works best around **6,000 Kbps**. YouTube can support much higher bitrate targets, especially at 1080p 60fps and above.

## Which platform is better for high bitrate streaming?

For high-resolution streaming, YouTube is usually the better fit. For standard 720p and 1080p live content, both can work well if you tune bitrate correctly.
