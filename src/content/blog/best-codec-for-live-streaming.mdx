The best codec for live streaming depends on your goals. **H.264** remains the default for compatibility, while **HEVC** and **AV1** offer better compression if your hardware and platform support them.

## H.264

- widest compatibility
- easiest safe default

## HEVC

- better compression than H.264
- not as universally supported

## AV1

- strongest efficiency
- best long-term option when supported
